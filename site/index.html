Networker Nightmares

Stories about defeat, disappointment, and disasters.

Every networker has stories to tell...

We're collecting your horror stories and will share them as a conference talk, probably at the next DENOG conference. This was inspired by the Sysadmin Nightmares talk at the Gulaschprogrammiernacht 2018. Unfortunatly, this talk was not streamed or recorded, so everyone could talk openly. Sysadmin Nightmares was accurately described1 as

self-help group therapy session

Ever had to remove a switch from a rack using an angle grinder? Found out that you're renting your own service from a reseller? Had a router that - literally - crashed? Had a discussion with TAC about whether the misbahaving line-card can actually mechanically fit in the chassis where it's running?

We try to be your poxy server, so nobody will ever know it was you! ;-)

Submit

We will collect your stories and compile a presenatation. You can submit

  • pictures
  • text (english/german - we might translate/summarize if needbe)
  • audio (if you want to the story yourself)
  • videos
or any combination of that. In most cases, pictures and a few sentences that explain what happened would be best. If you want to submit text/audio/video, please keep it short and don't turn it into a novel.

Only submit material that you allow us to publish in a conference talk! Please also tell us if you want to be named.
